01 — About

What is FBLA, anyway?

Future Business Leaders of America is the largest career-focused student business organization in the world. At North Forsyth, our chapter brings together students who want to sharpen their business chops, explore careers, and compete at regional, state, and national levels.

Whether you're into accounting, public speaking, web design, coding, marketing, or just want to build a network and a résumé that actually stands out — there's a place for you here.

Why join?

  • Compete in 75+ events that match your interests
  • Travel to regional, state, and national conferences
  • Network with business professionals & alumni
  • Lead — run for office, start a project, run a meeting
  • Earn scholarships, awards, and résumé-ready experience
